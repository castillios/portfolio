Software for Us

I'm currently a volunteer Software Engineer, contributing to projects that enhance our experiences and constantly biding my time to deepen my skillset. A 2025 grad with a B.S. in Computer Science and a minor in Digital Arts, I've found that the learning never ends for me.

These days, I've been spending my time working on projects that derive their information from human insights. To me, technology has always been a human forward field. It should not only enhance our personal experiences but act as an agent to work alongside with.

I've always been around computers growing up. And to me, being the bridge that closes the accessibility gap is truly my biggest goal in life.
